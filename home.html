About Us

Back history of our Village and Heritage




we are from south Indian State of Kerala.
Precisely Alleppey district known as Venice of the east, and our village is famous for world famous writer Thakazhy shivashankar Pillai.The village name is Thakazhy.

During 19th century portuguese where ruling a small south indian kingdom called Purakad. It was a famous port town for spices export at that time.


Later portuguese had a treaty arrengement with neighbouring country called Chembakashery and later they handed over their kingdom to Chembakashery and merged with it. In the treaty arrangement portuguese agreed to train the Chembakashery military with arms and ammunition. It was that time a modern weopon.


As part of the agreement the Chembakashery king has told them to settle down the officers to train his military. They have built few building for to live. This build is one among them and it is believed to be built in 1865. Since the roof tiles dating back to 1865. Now we are preserving the century old building as a colonial remnant.


We are using it as a heritage Tourist home if anyone want to visit.
